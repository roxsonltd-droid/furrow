Напомняне за **Furrow** (https://furrow-github.vercel.app):

1. Vercel → проект **furrow-github** → Environment Variables:
   - `RESEND_API_KEY`
   - `RESEND_FROM` (верифициран домейн)
   - `FURROW_INBOX_EMAIL`
2. **Redeploy**
3. Тест: https://furrow-github.vercel.app/register.html
